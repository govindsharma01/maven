Questions

Things people
usually ask us.

What is your minimum engagement period?

+

Our minimum engagement for retainer services is 3 months. Project-based work like websites or brand identity has no minimum term.

How quickly can you get started?

+

Once we've agreed on scope and received your onboarding payment, we typically begin within 48–72 hours. First campaigns can go live within the first week.

Is ad spend included in your pricing?

+

No — ad spend is billed separately and passed through at cost. We never mark up media spend. Our fee covers strategy, management, creative, and reporting only.

Do you work with brands outside India?

+

Yes — we work with brands across India, the UAE, and Southeast Asia. Our team is remote-friendly and has experience running multi-market campaigns simultaneously.

What makes Mavèn Studio different?

+

We are genuinely full-service — not a performance agency that outsources design, or a design agency that doesn't understand ads. Every service is in-house, which means faster turnaround, better integration, and one consistent voice across everything we build.

How do you report on results?

+

Every client receives a custom monthly report covering the metrics that matter — ROAS, CPL, organic growth, conversions. We also offer weekly check-in calls for retainer clients.
